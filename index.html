Welcome to Kelly Wood Builder

Kelly Wood Builder is a General Contractor founded in Amarillo, Texas and now with offices in McKinney, Texas. We operate throughout the Midwest region. Started in 1977, we have come to specialize in healthcare and retail construction, but remain very active in all types of projects. Our alliance with multiple architectural firms have paved the way to success in Design/Build, Construction Management, and Fast Track construction.
